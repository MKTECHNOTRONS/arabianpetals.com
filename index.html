FAQ'S

What does Arabian Petals offer as a flower shop ?

Arabian Petals is popular and No#1 shop in UAE for all floral products, including beautiful flower bouquets, delectable cakes, vibrant balloons, and delightful chocolates for any special occasion.

How can I order a flower bouquet from Arabian Petals?

Ordering is easy! Just visit the ArabianPetals website, select your preferred bouquet, and follow the simple steps to place your order.

Can I Customize A Bouquet Or Arrangement, flower box for a unique gift?

Of course! Arabian Petals allows you to customize your flower box, flower Bouquets and flower Arrangements adding a personal touch to your gift.

What Occasions Do Your Flowers Cater To?

Our flowers and gift hampers are perfect for all occasions, Arabian Petals have curated collections of goodies, perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or any celebration where you want to delight someone.

How can I locate a flower bouquet near me on your website?

To find a flower bouquet near your location, simply use our search or location feature on the website to discover the nearest options.

Do You Offer Same-Day Flower Delivery?

Yes, we provide same-day flower delivery for orders placed before 12PM to ensure your special moments are celebrated in a timely manner.

Do you offer flower delivery to Dubai, Sharjah, Abu Dhabi, Ajman and All Over UAE?

Yes, Arabian Petals provides flower delivery services to all over UAE, ensuring your gifts reach your loved ones no matter where they are. Please check our website to select the locations we currently serve.

Delivery Charges Are Different For Out Of Dubai.

Are Your Flowers Sourced Sustainably?

Our flowers are sourced from trusted growers who prioritizing sustainability and eco-friendly cultivation methods.

How Can I Track My Order?

Once your order is processed, we will provide a tracking number. You can use this number to monitor the progress of your delivery.

What Payment Options Do You Accept?

We accept payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, COD (Cash on Delivery), and other secure online payment options.

What is the lifespan of flowers and What precautions do you take for flower freshness?

Our flowers are sourced from the finest farms around the World and carry our freshness guarantee. We deliver flowers close bud so they are fresh for 7 days, and also ensure the freshness and quality of your flower arrangements during transportation.

Do You Offer A Guarantee For On Time Delivery And Good Condition?

Yes, we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you are not satisfied with your order, you can return it according to ourreturn policyand get a refund.
